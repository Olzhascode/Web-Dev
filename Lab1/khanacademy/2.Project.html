Switzerland is a breathtaking country located in the heart of the Alps. It is world-famous for its crystal-clear lakes, majestic mountain peaks, and high quality of life.
Whether it is the vibrant streets of Zurich or the peaceful valleys of the Bernese Oberland, Switzerland offers a perfect mix of modern luxury and untouched nature.
It is a dream destination for hikers and chocolate lovers alike.

Must-visit cities:

  • Zurich: Financial capital with great shopping.
  • Geneva: City of diplomacy on a beautiful lake.
  • Lucerne: Famous for its wooden bridge and old town.
  • Interlaken: The gateway to the Swiss Alps.

Turkey is a unique bridge between Europe and Asia, where ancient history meets stunning Mediterranean coasts. From the bustling bazaars and grand mosques of Istanbul to the surreal landscapes of Cappadocia,
this country never ceases to amaze. Visitors come for the warm hospitality, incredible food, and the turquoise waters of the Turkish Riviera. It is a place where every corner tells a story from a different era.

Must-visit cities:

  • Istanbul: Where East meets West.
  • Antalya: The heart of the Turkish Riviera.
  • Cappadocia: Famous for hot air balloons and cave dwellings.
  • Bodrum: Elegant white-washed coastal city.
